About Me

I've spent most of my adult life travelling Canada.
I've lived in 4 provinces, each one inspiring me in their own way

From big city living, to acreages kilometers from any large city, I spent 20 years in this absolutely beautiful, and ENORMOUS province. The people, the culture, the thriving major cities, these are the key elements to who I am deep at my core.

Montreal, a city with people from all walks of life, jaw-dropping scenery and eye-popping architecture.
This spectacular city opened my life to adventure, independance and the ability to wear my heart on my sleeve

At 23 years old, I packed my car with what belongings I could manage to bring, and spent nearly one third of my life in Alberta. From the extreme work lifestyle, to self-reliance, this province is easily responsible for shaping me into the hard-working man I am today

B.C. feels like home, something I've searched for for a long time. I'm proud to be a resident of this breath-taking province. It's where I learned to slow my fast-paced life to enjoy the small things, taking time to learn coding which has given me new opportunities in life.
