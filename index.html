About Me

I've spent most of my adult life travelling Canada.
I've lived in 4 provinces, each one inspiring me in their own way

From big city living, to acreages kilometers from any large city, I spent 20 years in this absolutely beautiful, and ENORMOUS province. This is where it can all be traced back to. The people, the culture, the thriving major cities, these are the key elements to who I am deep at my core.

Don't let their language intimidate you! It is the language of romance and love after all!
This province holds a special place in my heart. Montreal, a city with people from all walks of life, surrounded by jaw-dropping scenery and eye-popping architecture, this popular eastern city is a must visit for any Canadian looking to explore within their own country. Besides the beauty, this spectacular city saved my life in many ways, opening my future to adventure, independance and most importantly, the ability to wear my heart on my sleeve

Hitting the open road with my car packed with what little belongings I could fit with me, Alberta was a key part of my life, spending nearly 1 third of my life there. From the extreme work lifestyle, fortifying my already strong work ethic, to the solo life skills developed to endure being 4000+ kilometers from any family, this province is easily responsible for shaping me into the man I am today

I made it! I came back in 2017 and don't ever plan on leaving! It's difficult to explain to locals what appeals to me the most here. I find myself often summing it up to "I feel at home finally".
