About Me

I've spent most of my adult life travelling Canada.
I've lived in 4 provinces, each one inspiring me in their own way

From big city living, to acreages kilometers from any large city, I spent 20 years in this absolutely beautiful, and ENORMOUS province.
This is where it can all be traced back to. The people, the culture, the thriving major cities, these are the key elements to who I am deep at my core.

This province holds a special place in my heart. Montreal, a city with people from all walks of life, jaw-dropping scenery and eye-popping architecture.
This spectacular city saved my life in many ways, opening my future to adventure, independance and the ability to wear my heart on my sleeve

At 23 years old, I packed my car with what belongings I could manage to bring, and spent nearly one third of my life in Alberta.
From the extreme work lifestyle, to self-reliance, this province is easily responsible for shaping me into the hard-working man I am today

Returning in 2017, years after promising myself I would root myself here, I made it official by purchasing my first home here!
It's difficult to explain why I chose the island. I find myself often summing it up to finally feeling at home. The scenery, ocean and mountains are a nice plus!
